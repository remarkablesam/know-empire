How We Make Money (Without Hurting Users)

Sustainable Growth Model

Know Empire is free to join and start trading. Our revenue model is designed to be fair and fuel platform development.

  • ✅ Free to join
  • 🤝 2% fee on *completed* trades (only when value is exchanged!)
  • 🔒 Staking optional (more below)

Funds are used for rewards, growth, and continuous development of the platform.

Staking = Skin in the Game

Commitment & Trust

Staking $KNOW tokens before a trade is optional but signals serious commitment to the exchange, enhancing trust between users.

  • 🔒 Staking is optional but shows commitment
  • 🔄 Refunded after trade completion & mutual feedback
  • 🚀 Top stakers get boosts & exclusive perks
  • 🔥 0.2% of staked $KNOW burned, 1.8% to treasury (if trade fee applies)
